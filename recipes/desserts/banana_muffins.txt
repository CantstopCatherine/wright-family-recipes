Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 bananas
- 1 egg
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
2. Cream butter, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla
3. Add bananas and egg and beat until combined
4. Add in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon
5. Beat just until combined
6. Scoop into muffin tins
7. Bake for 25 minutes, makes ~12
