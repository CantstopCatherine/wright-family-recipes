Ingredients:
- 10 cups apples, peeled and sliced
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tablspoon flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 cup quick oats
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 cup melted butter

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
2. Mix sliced apples, sugar, and 1 tablespoon flour
3. Spread evenly into a a 9x13 pan
4. Pour water over mixture in pan
5. Combine oats, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and melted butter
6. Crumbly evenly over apples
7. Bake for ~45 minutes
