Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 2/4 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup canola oil
- 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Struessel
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup flour
- 1/4 cup cold butter

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
3. Add egg, milk, oil and beat well
4. Fold in blueberries
5. Spread into greased 9 inch square pan
6. In another bowl, combine sugar, flour and cut in butter until crumbly
7. Sprinkle mixture over batter
8. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until cake pick comes out clean
