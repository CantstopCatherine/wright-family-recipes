Ingredients:
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup confectionary sugar, sifted
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 2 1/2 cups flour, sifted

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F
2. Cream butter well
3. Add vanilla + sugar and mix well
4. Add flour and salt and mix well
5. Roll into teaspoon sized balls and placed on greased cookie sheet
6. Bake for 15  to 20 minutes or until bottoms lightly brown
7. Cool mostly but while still slightly warm, roll in confectionary sugar
