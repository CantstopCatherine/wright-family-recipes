Ingredients:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup canola oil
- 1 cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon lemo extract
- 3 eggs
- 4 1/2 to 5 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

Icing
- 1 cup confectionary sugar
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon milk or water

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
2. Mix together oil, sugar, orange juice, eggs, and lemon extract
3. Sift dry ingredients
4. Add wet ingredients (batter will be sticky and soft)
5. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto sheets
6. Bake for 8-10 minutes
7. Makes 70 cookies
